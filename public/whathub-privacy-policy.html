Privacy Policy for WhatHub

Last Updated:

1. Introduction

WhatHub ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our iOS application WhatHub (the "App").

2. Information Collection and Use

WhatHub is designed with your privacy in mind:

  • We do not collect or store any personal information
  • We do not track your usage or behavior
  • We do not have access to your WhatsApp messages or media
  • All WhatsApp functionality is handled through official WhatsApp services

3. Local Device Storage

The following information may be stored locally on your device:

  • App preferences and settings
  • Authentication status
  • Theme preferences
  • Face ID/Touch ID settings
  • Custom PIN configurations

4. Security Features

We implement several security features to protect your privacy:

  • Biometric authentication (Face ID/Touch ID)
  • Custom PIN protection
  • Secure app lock functionality

5. Third-Party Services

Our App interfaces with WhatsApp services. Please refer to WhatsApp's privacy policy for information about how they handle your data.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

7. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify users of any material changes through the App or via the App Store.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@whathub.app
